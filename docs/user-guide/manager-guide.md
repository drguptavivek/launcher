## ðŸ“± Mobile App Usage for Regional Managers

### Field Visit Preparation
1. **Schedule visits** to different team locations
2. **Review team performance** before visits
3. **Prepare discussion points** and agenda items
4. **Charge devices** and download necessary data
5. **Confirm appointments** with field supervisors

- **Log in using your credentials** (same as all mobile users)
- **Demonstrate app usage** to team members
- **Collect real-time feedback** on app and process issues

- **Document observations** and recommendations
- **Create action items** for teams and supervisors
- **Schedule follow-up meetings** as needed
- **Upload collected data** and photos immediately
- **Share insights** with other regional managers
