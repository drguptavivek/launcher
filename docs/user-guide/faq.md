### **General Login Issues**

**Q: I forgot my PIN/password. What should I do?**
**A:**
- **Mobile App Users:** Contact your supervisor immediately for a PIN reset
- **Web Dashboard Users:** Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
- **Never share your login information** with coworkers, even if you trust them

**Q: Why am I locked out of my account?**
**A:** Accounts lock temporarily after too many failed login attempts for security:
- **Wait 30 minutes** for automatic unlock

- **Avoid repeated failed attempts** to prevent extended lockouts

**Q: Can I change my user code or email address?**
**A:**
- **User codes** are assigned by supervisors and generally cannot be changed
- **Email addresses** can be updated by system administrators
- **Contact your supervisor** for any account information changes

### **Multi-Device and Multi-Interface Access**

**Q: Can I use SurveyLauncher on multiple devices?**
**A:**
- **Field Workers:** Generally use only one assigned device for security
- **Supervisors/Managers:** May access both mobile app and web dashboard
- **Never share devices** or login credentials with others

**Q: Why can't I access the web dashboard?**
**A:**
- **Team Members** only have mobile app access by design
- **Supervisors, Managers, and Administrators** have web dashboard access
- **Check your role** in your profile or contact your supervisor

## üì± Mobile App Questions

### **App Functionality**

**Q: Does the app work without internet connection?**
**A:** Yes! The app is designed for offline work:
- **Continue working** on surveys and tasks
- **Data saves locally** on your device
- **Automatic upload** when internet connection returns
- **Check "Pending Uploads"** status when connected again

**Q: How much data does the app use?**
**A:** Data usage is minimal:
- **GPS tracking** uses very small amounts of data
- **Survey uploads** vary by size (text uses minimal data, photos use more)
- **Daily usage** typically less than 50MB for normal work
- **Use WiFi** when available to reduce mobile data usage

**Q: Can I use the app on my personal phone?**
**A:** Generally no, for security and data protection:
- **Organization devices** are pre-configured and secured
- **Personal devices** may not meet security requirements
- **Contact your supervisor** if special circumstances apply

### **GPS and Location**

**Q: Why isn't my GPS working?**
**A:** Common GPS issues and solutions:
- **Go outdoors** - GPS doesn't work well inside buildings
- **Enable location services** in Android settings
- **Check for clear sky view** - tall buildings block signals
- **Restart your device** if GPS stays red for more than 10 minutes
- **Move to higher ground** for better satellite reception

**Q: Is my location tracked all the time?**
**A:** No, location tracking is limited:
- **Only during work hours** that you're scheduled to work
- **Only when you're logged in** to the mobile app
- **Never on weekends or holidays** unless scheduled to work
- **Your privacy is protected** outside work hours

**Q: How accurate is the GPS tracking?**
**A:** GPS accuracy varies:
- **Green status:** Usually accurate within 10-20 feet
- **Yellow status:** Less accurate, within 50-100 feet
- **Red status:** No GPS signal available
- **Accuracy affects** location verification for task completion

### **Battery and Device**

**Q: My battery drains quickly. What can I do?**
**A:** Extend battery life with these tips:
- **Lower screen brightness** to 50% or less
- **Close other apps** running in background
- **Enable battery saver mode** during work
- **Turn off WiFi** when not using internet
- **Carry a power bank** for long work days
- **Keep device updated** for battery improvements

**Q: How much storage space does the app need?**
**A:** Storage requirements are minimal:
- **App itself:** About 50MB
- **Cached data:** Up to 200MB, automatically managed
- **Survey data:** Varies, typically less than 100MB monthly
- **Photos:** Largest storage user, depends on number and quality
