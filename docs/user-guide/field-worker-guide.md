## üîê Your First Login

### Step 1: Start the App
1. Find the **SurveyLauncher** app on your Android device
2. Tap to open it
3. You'll see the login screen

### Step 2: Enter Your Information
You'll need three things to log in:

1. **Device ID** (already filled in on your device)
2. **Your User Code** (like "EMP001" or "JS123" - get this from your supervisor)
3. **Your PIN** (6-digit number you create)

### Step 3: Create Your PIN (First Time Only)
1. Enter a **6-digit PIN** that you can remember
2. **Don't use obvious numbers** like 123456 or your birthdate
3. **Write it down somewhere safe** in case you forget
4. Confirm your PIN by entering it again

### Step 4: Complete Your Profile
1. Enter your **full name**
2. Confirm your **team assignment**
3. Review the **work schedule** for your team
4. Tap **"Complete Setup"**

**‚úÖ You're now ready to start working!**

## üó∫Ô∏è GPS and Location Tracking

### How GPS Tracking Works
- **Automatic tracking** starts when you log in during work hours
- **Location recorded** every 3 minutes while you're working
- **Only tracks during work hours** - Your privacy is respected outside work time
- **Needs clear sky view** for best accuracy

### What GPS is Used For
- **Safety** - Your supervisor knows you're safe during field work
- **Work verification** - Confirms you visited required locations
- **Time tracking** - Helps verify work hours and travel time
- **Emergency response** - Helps find you quickly if needed

### GPS Status Indicators
- **üü¢ Green** - Good GPS signal (accurate location)
- **üü° Yellow** - Weak GPS signal (location might be less accurate)
- **üî¥ Red** - No GPS signal (check your location settings)

### If GPS Isn't Working
1. **Check you're outdoors** - GPS doesn't work well inside buildings
2. **Enable location services** in your Android settings
3. **Restart the app** if GPS signal stays red
4. **Contact your supervisor** if problems continue

## üìã Completing Surveys and Tasks

### Starting a New Task
1. **Tap "Start New Task"** on your dashboard
2. **Select the survey** you need to complete
3. **Review the instructions** before starting
4. **Tap "Begin Survey"** to start

### During Surveys
- **Follow the on-screen instructions** carefully
- **Take photos** if requested (check permission prompts)
- **Answer all required questions** (marked with red asterisks *)
- **Save your progress** often if it's a long survey
- **Ask for help** if you don't understand a question

### Submitting Completed Work
1. **Review all your answers** before submitting
2. **Tap "Submit"** when you're finished
3. **Wait for confirmation** that your work was uploaded
4. **Check that the task** appears in "Completed" section

### If You Can't Complete a Task
1. **Save what you've done** so far
2. **Add a note** explaining why you couldn't finish
3. **Contact your supervisor** for guidance
4. **Return to the task** later if possible

## üÜò Getting Help and Emergency Situations

### Common Problems You Can Fix Yourself

#### **Can't Log In**
- **Check your user code** - Make sure it's correct
- **Try your PIN again** - Be careful with numbers
- **Restart the app** - Close and reopen it
- **Check internet connection** - WiFi or mobile data

#### **GPS Not Working**
- **Go outside** - GPS needs clear sky view
- **Check location settings** - Enable GPS in Android settings
- **Restart your device** - Turn off and on again
- **Move to higher ground** - Better GPS signal

#### **App Won't Upload Data**
- **Check internet connection** - Try WiFi if mobile data is weak
- **Wait a few minutes** - Sometimes uploads are delayed
- **Restart the app** - This often fixes upload problems
- **Check your storage** - Make sure device has enough space

### When to Contact Your Supervisor

**Contact your supervisor immediately if:**
- **Your device is lost or stolen**
- **You can't log in after trying several times**
- **The app shows error messages**
- **You're in an unsafe situation**
- **You need to work outside normal hours**

**How to contact your supervisor:**
- **Phone call** for urgent problems
- **Text message** for non-urgent issues
- **Email** for detailed explanations
- **In person** during team meetings

### Emergency Situations
**If you're in danger or need immediate help:**
1. **Call emergency services** first (911 or local emergency number)
2. **Contact your supervisor** as soon as it's safe
3. **Use the app's emergency feature** if available
4. **Follow your organization's safety procedures**

## üîã Battery and Device Management

### Keeping Your Device Working
- **Start each day with 80%+ battery** if possible
- **Carry a charger or power bank** for long work days
- **Close other apps** to save battery
- **Lower screen brightness** to extend battery life
- **Turn off WiFi** when not needed to save power

### Battery Saving Tips
- **Enable battery saver mode** during long work days
- **Keep apps updated** - Updates often include battery improvements
- **Restart device daily** - This helps battery performance
- **Avoid extreme temperatures** - Heat and cold damage batteries

### Low Battery Warnings
- **20% warning:** Start looking for a charger
- **10% warning:** Finish urgent tasks, charge soon
- **5% warning:** Save work and charge immediately

## üì∂ Internet and Connectivity

### Types of Internet Connection
- **WiFi:** Best for large data uploads, saves mobile data
- **Mobile Data (4G/5G):** Works everywhere, uses your data plan
- **No Internet:** App works offline, uploads when connected

### Working Without Internet
- **App continues working** with no internet
- **Data saves locally** on your device
- **Automatic upload** when internet is available again
- **Check upload status** when you get connection back

### Connection Problems
- **Try switching between WiFi and mobile data**
- **Move to different location** for better signal
- **Restart your device** if connection issues persist
- **Contact supervisor** if problems continue for hours
