## üîë Login and Authentication Issues

### Mobile App Login Problems

#### **"Invalid User Code" Error**
**Problem:** You can't log in because your user code isn't recognized.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check your user code spelling** - Look for typos or mixed-up numbers/letters
2. **Verify with your supervisor** that you have the correct user code
3. **Try a different device** if available to rule out device-specific issues
4. **Contact your supervisor** to confirm your account is active

**Still not working?** Your supervisor may need to reactivate your account.

#### **"Invalid PIN" Error**
**Problem:** Your PIN is being rejected, even though you're sure it's correct.

**Solutions:**
1. **Reset your PIN** with supervisor assistance
2. **Try logging in slowly** - be careful with number entries
3. **Check if CAPS LOCK is on** (if using letters in user code)
4. **Count your PIN digits** - make sure it's exactly 6 digits
5. **Wait 15 minutes** after multiple failed attempts (security lockout)

**PIN Reset Process:**
1. Contact your supervisor
2. Verify your identity
3. Receive temporary PIN or reset instructions
4. Create new 6-digit PIN
5. Test new PIN immediately

#### **"Device Not Found" Error**
**Problem:** The system doesn't recognize your device.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check your device ID** is correct and hasn't changed
2. **Restart your device** and try again
3. **Check internet connection** - try WiFi if mobile data isn't working
4. **Contact your supervisor** to verify device registration

### Web Dashboard Login Problems

#### **"Invalid Email or Password" Error**
**Problem:** Can't log into the web dashboard.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check email spelling** for typos
2. **Try the "Forgot Password"** link
3. **Clear browser cache** and cookies
4. **Try a different browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
5. **Check if account is locked** after multiple failed attempts

**Password Reset Process:**
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Create a new strong password
5. Log in with new password

#### **Account Locked Out**
**Problem:** Too many failed login attempts locked your account.

**Solutions:**
1. **Wait 30 minutes** for automatic unlock
2. **Contact your supervisor** for immediate unlock
3. **Request password reset** if you can't remember correct password
4. **Try logging in with correct credentials** after lockout period

## üì∂ Internet and Connection Issues

### Mobile App Connection Problems

#### **"No Internet Connection" Error**
**Problem:** App won't connect to upload data.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check mobile data** - turn off and on again
2. **Try WiFi connection** if available
3. **Move to better signal area**
4. **Restart your device** completely
5. **Check other apps** to confirm internet is working

**Working Without Internet:**
- App continues working normally
- Data saves locally on device
- Automatic upload when connection returns
- Check "Pending Uploads" status

#### **Slow Data Uploads**
**Problem:** Data is taking too long to upload.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check signal strength** - move to better coverage area
2. **Connect to WiFi** for faster uploads
3. **Wait a few minutes** - sometimes uploads are delayed
4. **Restart the app** - close completely and reopen
5. **Check device storage** - full storage slows uploads

#### **GPS Not Working**
**Problem:** GPS location isn't tracking or showing errors.

**Solutions:**
1. **Go outdoors** - GPS doesn't work well inside buildings
2. **Enable Location Services** in Android settings:
   - Settings ‚Üí Location ‚Üí Turn ON
   - Set accuracy to "High"
   - Allow SurveyLauncher app location access
3. **Restart your device**
4. **Check GPS status** in app status indicators
5. **Move to open area** with clear sky view

**GPS Status Indicators:**
- üü¢ **Green** - Good signal, accurate location
- üü° **Yellow** - Weak signal, less accurate
- üî¥ **Red** - No signal, location not available

### Web Dashboard Connection Problems

#### **Page Won't Load or Very Slow**
**Problem:** Web dashboard is slow or won't load completely.

**Solutions:**
1. **Refresh the page** (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. **Check internet connection** with other websites
3. **Clear browser cache** and cookies
4. **Try a different browser**
5. **Check if dashboard is down** for maintenance

**Browser Cache Clearing:**
- **Chrome:** Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear browsing data
- **Firefox:** Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear data
- **Safari:** Develop ‚Üí Empty caches (or Preferences ‚Üí Privacy)

## üîã Device and Battery Issues

### Battery Draining Too Fast

#### **Problem:** Device battery runs out quickly during work day.

**Solutions:**
1. **Lower screen brightness**
2. **Close other apps** running in background
3. **Enable battery saver mode**
4. **Turn off WiFi** when not needed
5. **Keep device updated** with latest software
6. **Carry a power bank** or extra charger

**Battery Saving Tips:**
- **Disable vibrations** for notifications
- **Use dark mode** if available
- **Reduce screen timeout** to 30 seconds
- **Close apps** when not actively using them
- **Avoid extreme temperatures** (hot/cold affects battery)

### Device Won't Turn On or Responds

#### **Problem:** Device is completely dead or frozen.

**Solutions:**
1. **Charge the device** for at least 30 minutes
2. **Try different charger** and cable
3. **Force restart** device (hold power button 10-15 seconds)
4. **Check for physical damage** - drops, water exposure
5. **Contact Device Manager** if device won't respond

**Emergency Data Recovery:**
- **Don't factory reset** without backup
- **Contact support immediately** for data recovery options
- **Document what happened** for insurance/warranty claims

## üìä App and Feature Problems

### Mobile App Issues

#### **App Crashes or Freezes**
**Problem:** SurveyLauncher app stops working or closes unexpectedly.

**Solutions:**
1. **Restart the app** - close and reopen
2. **Restart your device** completely
3. **Check app updates** in Play Store
4. **Clear app cache** in device settings
5. **Reinstall the app** as last resort

**App Update Process:**
1. Open Google Play Store
2. Search for "SurveyLauncher"
3. Tap "Update" if available
4. Test app after update completes

#### **Survey Questions Won't Load**
**Problem:** Survey questions are missing or won't display properly.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check internet connection**
2. **Refresh the survey** by pulling down to reload
3. **Restart the app** and try again
4. **Check survey availability** - may not be released yet
5. **Contact supervisor** if survey should be available

#### **Photos Won't Upload or Save**
**Problem:** Camera features not working or photos not saving.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check camera permissions** in app settings
2. **Ensure device storage** has space available
3. **Test device camera** outside the app
4. **Restart the app** after enabling permissions
5. **Check photo quality settings** - lower quality if needed

**Permission Check:**
1. Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí SurveyLauncher
2. Permissions ‚Üí Camera ‚Üí Allow
3. Permissions ‚Üí Storage ‚Üí Allow

### Web Dashboard Issues

#### **Reports Won't Generate or Load**
**Problem:** Dashboard reports are empty or won't load.

**Solutions:**
1. **Wait a few minutes** - large reports take time to load
2. **Check date range** - adjust to smaller time period
3. **Refresh the page** and try again
4. **Check your role permissions** - may not have report access
5. **Try different report** to see if it's specific to one report

#### **Team Member Information Missing**
**Problem:** Can't see team members or their information.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check team assignment** - you may be in wrong team
2. **Verify permissions** - ensure you have team access
3. **Refresh the page** to update data
4. **Check filter settings** - may be filtering out team members
5. **Contact system admin** if you should have access

### Working Hours Problems

#### **"Access Outside Working Hours" Message**
**Problem:** App won't let you work during what should be your work hours.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check your work schedule** in your profile
2. **Verify your time zone** is correct
3. **Check device time** matches actual time
4. **Ask supervisor** about schedule changes
5. **Request supervisor override** for emergency access

#### **Time Tracking Inaccuracies
**Problem:** Work hours aren't being recorded correctly.

**Solutions:**
1. **Log in and out properly** at start and end of work
2. **Check internet connection** during login/logout
3. **Don't leave app running** when not working
4. **Report discrepancies** to your supervisor
5. **Keep manual backup** of your work hours

### GPS Time Stamping Issues

#### **Location Times Don't Match Work Times
**Problem:** GPS timestamps don't match when you actually worked.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check device time settings** are correct
2. **Enable automatic time sync** in device settings
3. **Ensure good GPS signal** during work
4. **Report issues** to supervisor if persistent
5. **Document actual work times** separately

## üÜò Emergency and Override Issues

### Supervisor Override Not Working

#### **Problem:** Can't get supervisor override when needed.

**For Team Members:**
1. **Contact supervisor directly** by phone or message
2. **Try mobile app override feature** if available
3. **Explain the emergency** clearly and why override is needed
4. **Document the situation** for compliance

**For Supervisors:**
1. **Check supervisor PIN** is correct and active
2. **Verify team member identity** before approving
3. **Document override reason** and duration
4. **Monitor override usage** for abuse prevention

#### **Override Duration Too Short**
**Problem:** Supervisor override expires before work is completed.

**Solutions:**
1. **Request additional override time** from supervisor
2. **Plan work more efficiently** within override periods
3. **Start override request earlier** in the work session
4. **Document why additional time** was needed

## üìû When to Contact Support

### Self-Service First
Before contacting support, try these steps:

1. **Restart your device** - fixes 40% of problems
2. **Check internet connection** - fixes 30% of problems
3. **Update the app** - fixes 15% of problems
4. **Clear cache/data** - fixes 10% of problems
5. **Check permissions** - fixes 5% of problems

### Contact Support If:

**Urgent Issues (Contact Immediately):**
- **Device lost or stolen**
- **Safety emergency involving team member**
- **System completely down** for everyone
- **Data security concerns**

**Same-Day Issues (Contact During Work Hours):**
- **Can't log in** after trying all troubleshooting steps
- **Critical survey deadline** at risk due to technical problems
- **Multiple team members** experiencing same issue
- **Data submission failures** affecting project timeline

**Non-Urgent Issues (Contact When Convenient):**
- **Minor app bugs** that don't prevent work
- **Feature requests** or improvement suggestions
- **Documentation questions**
- **Training requests** for new features

### Information to Have Ready

When contacting support, have this information ready:

**Basic Information:**
- **Your name and role**
- **Your team assignment**
- **Device ID** (for mobile app issues)
- **Browser and version** (for web issues)

**Problem Details:**
- **What exactly is happening** - be specific
- **When did it start** - date and time
- **What you've tried** - list troubleshooting steps attempted
- **Error messages** - exact text if available
- **Impact on work** - how it affects your ability to complete tasks

**Context Information:**
- **Are others affected** or just you
- **Has this happened before**
- **Recent changes** to device, app, or network
- **Urgency level** and deadline concerns

## üîß Advanced Troubleshooting

### Device Specific Solutions

#### **Android-Specific Issues:**
- **Safe Mode**: Restart holding volume down button to test if apps are causing issues
- **Developer Options**: Enable for advanced debugging (only if technically comfortable)
- **Factory Reset**: Last resort - backup data first!

#### **Network-Specific Issues:**
- **APN Settings**: Check mobile data configuration with carrier
- **VPN Conflicts**: Disable VPN if causing connection problems
- **Firewall Issues**: Check if network is blocking SurveyLauncher

### Data Recovery Solutions

#### **Lost Survey Data:**
- **Check app local storage** for saved drafts
- **Contact support immediately** for server-side recovery
- **Re-create critical data** if recovery isn't possible
- **Document what was lost** for quality assurance

#### **Backup and Sync Issues:**
- **Manual sync** option in app settings
- **Export data** to local storage as backup
- **Verify cloud storage** quotas and permissions
- **Schedule regular data exports** for important projects
