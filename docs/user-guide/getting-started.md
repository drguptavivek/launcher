## üì± Step 2: Set Up Your Access

### For Mobile App Users

#### 1. Get Your Android Device
- Your organization will provide a configured Android device
- The SurveyLauncher app should already be installed
- Make sure the device is charged and has internet connection

#### 2. Find Your Login Information
You'll need:
- **Device ID** (already on your device)
- **User Code** (like "EMP123" - from your supervisor)
- **Team Assignment** (which team you belong to)

#### 3. First-Time Login
1. **Open the SurveyLauncher app**
2. **Enter your User Code**
3. **Create a 6-digit PIN** (choose something memorable but secure)
4. **Confirm your PIN** by entering it again
5. **Complete your profile** with your name and team information

### For Web Dashboard Users

#### 1. Access the Web Dashboard
- Open a web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Go to your organization's SurveyLauncher web address
- Example: `https://yourcompany.surveylauncher.app`

#### 2. First-Time Login
1. **Enter your email address**
2. **Click "First Time Setup"**
3. **Check your email** for setup instructions
4. **Create a strong password** (at least 8 characters, mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
5. **Log in** with your new password

#### 3. Complete Your Profile
1. **Verify your role and team assignment**
2. **Set up security questions** (if prompted)
3. **Review system notifications** settings
4. **Explore the dashboard** to see your available features

---

## üîê Step 3: Security Setup

### Create Strong Credentials

#### For Mobile App PIN
- **6 digits long**
- **Don't use obvious numbers** (123456, 000000, your birthdate)
- **Don't use repeating numbers** (111111, 222222)
- **Write it down somewhere safe** in case you forget

#### For Web Dashboard Password
- **At least 8 characters**
- **Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters**
- **Include numbers and special characters** (!@#$%^&*)
- **Don't use personal information** (name, birthdate, phone)

### Security Best Practices
- **Never share your login information** with anyone
- **Log out when finished** using the system
- **Keep your contact information** up to date
- **Report suspicious activity** immediately

## üó∫Ô∏è Step 8: Location and GPS Setup

### For Mobile App Users
1. **Enable GPS/Location services** in Android settings
2. **Set location accuracy to "High"**
3. **Allow SurveyLauncher to access location** during work hours only
4. **Test GPS tracking** by walking around briefly

### Privacy Information
- **Location tracked only** during scheduled work hours
- **No tracking** outside work time or on weekends
- **Location data used for** safety, work verification, and emergency response
- **Your privacy is protected** by system policies

### If GPS Doesn't Work
- **Check you're outdoors** (GPS doesn't work well inside)
- **Enable location services** in Android settings
- **Restart the app** and try again
- **Contact your supervisor** if problems continue
