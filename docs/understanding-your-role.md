### **Understanding Your Scope**

#### **Team Members**
- **Can see:** Your own tasks, your team's information
- **Can work on:** Projects assigned to your team
- **Cannot see:** Other teams' work, system settings

#### **Field Supervisors**
- **Can see:** Your team's work, team member schedules, basic reports
- **Can work on:** Projects assigned to teams you supervise
- **Cannot see:** Other regions' work, system administration

#### **Regional Managers**
- **Can see:** All teams in your region, regional reports, performance data
- **Can work on:** Any project in your region, manage regional teams
- **Cannot see:** Other regions' work (unless you have special permissions)

#### **System Administrators**
- **Can see:** Everything in the system
- **Can work on:** Any project, any team, system-wide tasks
- **Your superpower:** Complete system access for maintenance and setup

### **Common Questions:**

**Q: "I can't log into the web dashboard, why?"**
A: Team Members only use the mobile app. Check if you have the right role for web access.

**Q: "I can see a project but can't work on it, why?"**
A: You might be able to see regional projects but can only work on portions assigned to you or your team.

**Q: "My supervisor can see my location, is that normal?"**
A: Yes, supervisors can see team member locations during work hours for safety and coordination.

**Q: "How do I get access to more features?"**
A: Talk to your supervisor about your role and responsibilities. Different roles have different access levels.
